My Top Three Movies

  1. A Walk To Remember

    2. My all time favorite romantic drama based on Nicholas Sparks' novel of the same name. The film follows Landon Carter, a rebellious high school senior, who falls in love with Jamie Sullivan, a kind and deeply spiritual girl with a terminal illness. Their unexpected romance transforms Landon's outlook on life, teaching him the power of love, faith, and redemption. Filled with heartfelt moments and emotional depth, the movie remains a touching tale of love and personal growth.

  2. The Lion King

    3. This is is a beloved Disney animated film that follows Simba, a young lion prince destined to become king. After the tragic death of his father, Mufasa, Simba is manipulated by his uncle Scar into fleeing the Pride Lands. With the help of friends Timon and Pumbaa, he grows up in exile before returning to reclaim his rightful place as king. Filled with breathtaking animation, unforgettable music, and powerful themes of responsibility and courage, The Lion King remains a timeless classic.
